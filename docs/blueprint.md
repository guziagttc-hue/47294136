# **App Name**: TechShop BD

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Browsing: Users can browse products by category, view featured items, and explore best-selling products displayed on the homepage.
- Product Search: An intuitive search bar allowing users to find specific products using keywords, brands, or product names.
- Detailed Product Pages: Dedicated pages for each product showcasing high-quality images, comprehensive descriptions, pricing, and technical specifications, along with an 'Add to Cart' button.
- Shopping Cart Management: Users can add products to their cart, modify quantities, or remove items before proceeding to checkout.
- Promotional Banners & Sliders: A dynamic hero banner to highlight ongoing offers, new arrivals, and key promotions to capture user attention immediately.
- Customer Inquiry Form: A simple contact form on the 'Contact Us' page, allowing users to submit queries, feedback, or support requests, integrated with Google Maps for location display.
- Product Content Database: A database to store and manage all product details, categories, images, pricing, and general website content such as About Us and Contact information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Professional Navy Blue (#001F3F) to establish trust and a sophisticated tech aesthetic for navigation and core branding.
- Background color: Clean and crisp white (#FFFFFF) for an uncluttered and modern look across all pages.
- Accent color: Vibrant Golden Yellow (#FFBF00) for calls-to-action like 'Add to Cart' and other important highlights, drawing user attention effectively.
- Font choice: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headlines and body text to ensure excellent readability and a consistent, contemporary, and objective tone suitable for a tech e-commerce site.
- Responsive layout: The website will feature a clean, modular grid-based design for product displays, ensuring seamless adaptability across all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop) for optimal user experience.
- Icon style: Simple, circular, and clearly identifiable icons for product categories, search, shopping cart, and wishlist to enhance navigation and visual clarity.
- Subtle interactive animations: Incorporate delicate hover effects for product cards and buttons, and smooth transitions for hero banners and sliders, to provide a dynamic yet non-distracting user experience.
